CANONICAL HERO JOURNEY (STRUCTURAL CORE)
(Status: Descriptive, closed, non-moral)

This list names the load-bearing components of the archetypal hero journey.
Items are structural, not stylistic. Order is typical, not mandatory.

------------------------------------------------------------
DEPARTURE
------------------------------------------------------------

1. Ordinary World
   A stable but insufficient situation.

2. Call
   Disruption, lack, or pull toward change.

3. Resistance
   Hesitation, refusal, or cost-awareness.

4. Threshold
   Commitment beyond easy return.

------------------------------------------------------------
TRANSFORMATION
------------------------------------------------------------

5. Trials
   Testing, learning, misfit, allies, opposition.

6. Ordeal
   Confrontation with loss, dissolution, or death-equivalent.

7. Gain
   Insight, capacity, object, or reorientation.

------------------------------------------------------------
RETURN
------------------------------------------------------------

8. Return Path
   Re-entry is non-trivial.

9. Integration
   Applying the gain to life or world.

10. Changed World
    The world or the hero is now different.

------------------------------------------------------------
MOGRI NOTE
------------------------------------------------------------

If further subdivision, symbolism, or role-adding
reduces clarity rather than increasing it,
the analysis is mogrifying and should stop.

------------------------------------------------------------
END OF LIST
