
Period-correct invented language: What's available and when?

Quick cheat sheet:

>2M: emotional calls

2M–200k: richer calls, no style

200k–70k: speech + mockery + flair

70k+: anything goes

=====================================

# PALEO-SPEECH CHEAT (MEDIA MODE)
# time bucket + allowed sound inventory
# rule: withhold sounds, never invent new ones

>2_000_000_YA:   # chimping / pre-speech

NO_FRICATIVES:
  f v s z ʃ ʒ θ ð x ɣ h

NO_LIQUIDS:
  l r ɾ ɹ ʀ

NO_GLIDES:
  j w

NO_SCHWA_OR_REDUCTION:
  ə ɐ ɪ ʊ ɛ ɔ

NO_AFFRICATES:
  tʃ dʒ ts dz

NO_CLUSTERS:
  CC (any consonant+consonant onset or coda)
  CCC (absolutely not)

NO_TIMING_TRICKS:
  contrastive stress
  vowel length contrasts
  deliberate rhythm
  syllable counting

ALLOWED (for sanity check):
  vowels:        a (primary), u (edge)
  consonants:    m n p b t d k g
  structure:     CV (maybe CVC if emotional)


2M_200K_YA:      # more sounds, still no style
  vowels:        a i u
  vowels_edge:   e o
  consonants:    m n p b t d k g
  edge:          s f l r (unstable, non-contrastive)
  no:            ʃ θ ð v z clusters schwa
  structure:     CV CVC (hesitant CC only)
  note:          richer calls, no ornament, no phonetic play

200K_70K_YA:     # speech unlock / mockery possible
  vowels:        a e i o u
  vowels_edge:   ə (unstressed, emerging)
  consonants:    p b t d k g m n s f ʃ l r
  edge:          v z θ ð
  clusters:      pl tr kl str skr ʃr
  structure:     CV CVC CCV CVCC
  note:          style visible, accents mockable, flair allowed

70K_YA_PLUS:     # modern speech
  vowels:        full (incl schwa diphthongs reduction)
  consonants:    full modern inventory
  clusters:      unrestricted
  structure:     unrestricted
  note:          only cultural constraints remain

# PRODUCTION RULES
# - audiences forgive missing sounds
# - audiences do NOT forgive premature precision
# - if it sounds like acting class before 200k, delete it
# - if someone can be teased for pronunciation, you are post-200k
