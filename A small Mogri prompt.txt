Tight cores (ascending density):

1. Still readable (67 bytes)

MOGRI=min-container(intent,across-prompts,anti-drift,pre-entity)

2. Trim connectors (59 bytes)

MOGRI=min-container(intent,across,anti-drift,pre-entity)

3. Symbol compression (44 bytes)

MOGRI=minC(intent,across,anti-drift,preE)

4. Higher density, still parseable (39 bytes)

MOGRI=minC(intent,across,antiD,preE)

5. Near-max compression (structured, not cryptic) (39 bytes) RECOMMENDED VERSION

MOGRI=minC(intent|across|antiD|preE)

6. Max density before readability starts to break (26 bytes)

MOGRI=minC(I|A|!D|preE)

(I=intent, A=across, !D=anti-drift, preE=pre-entity)
