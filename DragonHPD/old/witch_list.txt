-----------------------------------
EARNED CUTS
-----------------------------------
(we see them become it on screen)

- Circe
  Princess-by-birth, witch-by-practice.
  Exit never resolves; she keeps the island.

- Medea
  Princess first, witch later.
  Pays the cost of both.

- Daenerys Targaryen
  Princess framing, witch methods.
  The drift is shown, not excused.

- San (Princess Mononoke)
  Princess by role, witch by alignment with the non-human.
  Earned by refusing the crown.

- Maleficent (mythic reading)
  Fairy/princess adjacency → witch through betrayal.
  Shown, not explained.

-----------------------------------
ENTRY CUTS
-----------------------------------
(we meet them already composite)

- Morgan le Fay
  Sister, sorceress, queen-adjacent.
  No origin scene.

- Baba Yaga
  Crone or queen-maker depending on need.
  Princess energy without throne.

- Melisandre
  Court-access priestess with witch power.
  Origin deliberately withheld.

- Galadriel
  Already both when met.
  Power framed as restraint.

- The Lady of the Lake
  Grants kingship, lives outside it.

-----------------------------------
EXIT CUTS
-----------------------------------
(the composite is resolved by removal)

- Cassandra
  Princess with witch-knowledge.
  Exit via disbelief.

- Ariel (original Andersen)
  Witch bargain, princess hope.
  Exit through loss of voice/body.

- The White Witch (Narnia)
  Queen-witch fused.
  Exit by winter ending.

- Guinevere (some tellings)
  Queen role, witch consequences.
  Exit into silence/convent.

- Snow White’s Stepmother
  Queen frame, witch act.
  Exit by ritual punishment.

-----------------------------------
COMMON THREADS
-----------------------------------
- Appear at transition points
- Manage thresholds (law/magic, human/nonhuman)
- Resolution is optional; containment is common
- When society stabilises, they are removed
- When society destabilises, they are necessary
