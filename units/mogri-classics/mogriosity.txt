Mogriosity

Mogriosity is the degree to which a Mogri holds.

It describes how effectively a minimal container preserves framework intent across prompts without drift or invariant loss.

High mogriosity:

* structure holds
* intent persists
* no reinterpretation creep

Low mogriosity:

* drift present
* invariants weakening or lost
* structure being reinterpreted or bypassed

Mogriosity is not the container itself.
It is a property of a system using Mogri.

Mogri remains binary (present / absent).
Mogriosity is continuous (strong → weak).

Use:

“This session has high mogriosity.”
“Mogriosity dropped after turn 12.”
“That rewrite reduced mogriosity.”

Mogri defines the container.
Mogriosity describes how well it holds.
