Mogrience

Mogrience is the capacity of a system to sense and track its own structure under Mogri.

It describes internal awareness of whether framework intent is being preserved or drifting.

High mogrience:

* deviations detected early
* structure monitored during generation
* invariant breaches noticed

Low mogrience:

* drift goes unnoticed
* structure changes without detection
* invariants fail silently

Mogrience does not enforce Mogri.
It observes it.

Mogri defines the container.
Mogrience detects its state.
