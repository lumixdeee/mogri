Mogristence

Mogristence is the durability of Mogri over time and across transformations.

It describes how well a container continues to hold under pressure, length, or reinterpretation.

High mogristence:

* structure persists across many turns
* intent survives rewrites and expansions
* invariants remain intact under load

Low mogristence:

* structure degrades over time
* intent weakens with each transformation
* invariants erode under pressure

Mogristence does not define the container.
It describes how long it holds.

Mogri defines the container.
Mogristence describes its persistence.
