Lao-Mogri-Audit.txt
==================

Language: Lao
ISO code: lo
Native speakers consulted: everyday usage
Date: 2026-01

Register scope:
- everyday
- oral

CORE QUESTION
-------------
Does Lao provide a neutral lexical carrier for persistence-through-change?

CANDIDATE TERMS
---------------
- ເປັນ (pen)
- ກາຍເປັນ (kai pen)
- ປ່ຽນ (pian)

WHY THEY FAIL
-------------
- pen implies being or becoming
- kai pen implies becoming-into
- pian implies change event
- meaning strongly context-dependent

COMMON WORKAROUNDS USED INSTEAD
-------------------------------
- serial verbs
- aspectual particles
- pragmatic inference

COST OF WORKAROUND
------------------
Low to medium

VERDICT
-------
Mogri present? no

NOTES
-----
Lao parallels Thai closely in structure and limitations.
