CORE QUESTION
-------------
Does Sinhala provide a neutral lexical carrier for persistence-through-change?

CANDIDATE TERMS
---------------
- වෙන්න (venna)
- වෙනස් වීම (venas vīma)

WHY THEY FAIL
-------------
- venna implies becoming
- venas vīma implies change or difference
- verbs encode transition rather than persistence
