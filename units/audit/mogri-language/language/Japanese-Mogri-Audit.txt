CORE QUESTION
-------------
Does Japanese lexicalise persistence-through-change?

CANDIDATE TERMS
---------------
- なる (naru)
- 変わる (kawaru)

WHY THEY FAIL
-------------
- naru implies becoming-into
- kawaru implies a change event
- heavy reliance on context over lexical carriers

COMMON WORKAROUNDS USED INSTEAD
-------------------------------
- aspectual auxiliaries
- omission
- situational inference

COST OF WORKAROUND
------------------
Low to medium (context absorbs load)

VERDICT
-------
Mogri present? no

NOTES
-----
Japanese deliberately avoids pinning processes lexically.

STATUS
------
☑ Still missing mogri
