CORE QUESTION
-------------
Does Portuguese provide a neutral lexical carrier for persistence-through-change?

CANDIDATE TERMS
---------------
- tornar-se
- transformar-se
- vir a ser
- devir

WHY THEY FAIL
-------------
- tornar-se implies a state change
- transformar-se implies an operation
- vir a ser implies future arrival
- devir is abstract and technical

COMMON WORKAROUNDS USED INSTEAD
-------------------------------
- reflexive verb forms
- periphrastic constructions
- aspectual framing
- metaphorical language

COST OF WORKAROUND
------------------
High

VERDICT
-------
Mogri present? no

NOTES
-----
Portuguese exhibits the same pattern as Spanish and French,
with strong expressive workarounds but no neutral carrier.
