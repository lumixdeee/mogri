CORE QUESTION
-------------
Does Nepali lexicalise persistence-through-change neutrally?

CANDIDATE TERMS
---------------
- हुनु (hunu)
- बन्नु (bannu)
- बदलिनु (badlinu)

WHY THEY FAIL
-------------
- hunu implies being
- bannu implies becoming-into
- badlinu implies change
- light verb constructions imply endpoints

-------------------------------

- aspectual particles
- discourse continuity
