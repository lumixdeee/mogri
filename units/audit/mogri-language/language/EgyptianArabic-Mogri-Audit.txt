CORE QUESTION
-------------
Does Egyptian Arabic provide a neutral lexical carrier for persistence-through-change?

CANDIDATE TERMS
---------------
- بقى (baʔa)
- اتحول (itḥawwal)
- اتغير (itghayyar)

WHY THEY FAIL
-------------
- baʔa implies becoming or remaining
- itḥawwal implies transformation
- itghayyar implies change event
- pragmatic markers imply outcomes

- discourse particles
- narrative context
