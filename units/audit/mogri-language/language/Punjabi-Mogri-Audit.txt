CORE QUESTION
-------------
Does Punjabi provide a neutral lexical carrier for persistence-through-change?

CANDIDATE TERMS
---------------
- ਹੋਣਾ (hoṇā)
- ਬਣਨਾ (banṇā)
- ਬਦਲਣਾ (badalṇā)

WHY THEY FAIL
-------------
- hoṇā implies being or becoming
- banṇā implies becoming-into
- badalṇā implies change
- light verbs encode endpoints

COMMON WORKAROUNDS USED INSTEAD
-------------------------------
- compound verb constructions
- aspectual particles
- contextual framing
