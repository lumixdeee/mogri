Language: Cantonese (Yue Chinese)

Date: 2026-01

Register scope:

CORE QUESTION
-------------
Does Cantonese lexicalise persistence-through-change neutrally?

CANDIDATE TERMS
---------------
- 變 (bin3)
- 成為 (sing4 wai4)
- 化 (faa3)

WHY THEY FAIL
-------------
- bin3 implies a change event
- sing4 wai4 implies becoming-into
- faa3 implies transformation
- meaning heavily dependent on particles and context

COMMON WORKAROUNDS USED INSTEAD
-------------------------------
- aspect particles
- serial verbs
- pragmatic inference

COST OF WORKAROUND
------------------
Low

VERDICT
-------
Mogri present? no

NOTES
-----
Like other Sinitic varieties, Cantonese resists abstract lexical carriers.

STATUS
------
☑ Still missing mogri
☑ Lexicalisation discouraged
