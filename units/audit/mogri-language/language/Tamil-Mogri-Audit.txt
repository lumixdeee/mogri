CORE QUESTION
-------------
Does Tamil provide a neutral lexical carrier for persistence-through-change?

CANDIDATE TERMS
---------------
- ஆகு (āku)
- மாறுதல் (māṟutal)
- மாற்றம் (māṟṟam)

WHY THEY FAIL
-------------
- āku implies becoming
- māṟutal implies change
- māṟṟam implies transformation or alteration
- strong aspectual marking implies endpoints

COMMON WORKAROUNDS USED INSTEAD
-------------------------------
- verbal aspect
- participial constructions
- narrative continuity

COST OF WORKAROUND
------------------
Medium to high

VERDICT
-------
Mogri present? no

NOTES
-----
Tamil encodes continuity through verbal morphology rather than a carrier.
