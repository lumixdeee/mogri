CORE QUESTION
-------------
Does Thai lexicalise persistence-through-change as a neutral carrier?

CANDIDATE TERMS
---------------
- เป็น (pen)
- กลายเป็น (klai pen)
- เปลี่ยน (plian)

WHY THEY FAIL
-------------
- pen implies being or becoming
- klai pen implies becoming-into
- plian implies a change event
- strong reliance on aspect and context

COMMON WORKAROUNDS USED INSTEAD
-------------------------------
- serial verb constructions
- aspectual particles
- contextual inference

COST OF WORKAROUND
------------------
Low to medium

VERDICT
-------
Mogri present? no

NOTES
-----
Thai prioritises situational clarity over abstract carriers.
