CORE QUESTION
-------------
Does Kannada lexicalise persistence-through-change neutrally?

CANDIDATE TERMS
---------------
- ಆಗು (āgu)
- ಬದಲಾಗು (badalāgu)

WHY THEY FAIL
-------------
- āgu implies becoming
- badalāgu implies change
- verbs encode transition, not neutral persistence
