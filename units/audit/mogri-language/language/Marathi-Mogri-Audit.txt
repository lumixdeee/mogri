CORE QUESTION
-------------
Does Marathi provide a neutral lexical carrier for persistence-through-change?

CANDIDATE TERMS
---------------
- होणे (hoṇe)
- बनणे (banaṇe)
- बदलणे (badalṇe)

WHY THEY FAIL
-------------
- hoṇe implies being or becoming
- banaṇe implies becoming-into
- badalṇe implies change
- light verbs encode outcomes

COMMON WORKAROUNDS USED INSTEAD
-------------------------------
- compound verbs
- aspectual particles
- discourse continuity

------------------
Medium
